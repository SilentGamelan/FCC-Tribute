shôben no

taki wo mishô zo

naku kawazu

let me show you

my piss waterfall,

croaking frog!

小便の滝を見せうぞ鳴蛙

(written after his daughter's death)


Tsuyu no yo wa

tsuyu no yo nagara

sari nagara

the world of dew -

a world of dew indeed,

and yet, and yet . . .

露の世は露の世ながらさりながら

yare utsuna

hae ga te wo suri

ashi wo suru

don't swat the fly!

praying with his tiny hands

praying with feet

やれ打つな蝿が手をすり足をする

okuribi ya

taku mane shite

mo aki [no] tsuyu

like bonfires for the dead

burning to nothing...

autumn dew

送り火や焚く真似しても秋の露

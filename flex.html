The life of Kobayashi Issa

matte iru

saishi mo nai ka

tôshi-gamo

no wife and kids

waiting for you?

lingering duck.

待て居る妻子もないか通し鴨

neta inu ni fuwa

to kabusaru hito

ha kana

gently, a hat...

a leaf

寝た犬にふはとかぶさる一葉哉

yama neko mo

koi wa itasu yo

kado nozoki

even the wild cat

looks for sex...

peeking in the gate
